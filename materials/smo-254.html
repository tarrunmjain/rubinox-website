Rubinox Metal and Alloys is an India-based stockist, supplier, trader, importer and exporter of 254 SMO (6Mo) stainless steel, headquartered in Kalbadevi, Mumbai. 254 SMO is a super-austenitic grade known for excellent resistance to pitting and crevice corrosion in seawater and chloride-rich environments, where 316L may not be sufficient.
